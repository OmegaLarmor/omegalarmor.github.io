Spell Spiel

My first (and current favorite!) game project

Spell Spiel is an RPG typing game. You control Beam, the spellcasting main guy, and need to defeat a slew of monsters to defend your magic school!

An ambitious project, built as a team

(Play it!)

Oh man, I'm gonna have to keep this short. Spell Spiel was a 2 month project I made during the summer of 2017. I pitched the idea to the Gamkedo family and built it while leading a team of 10 people (11 if I include myself). In total, I must have done about 75-80% of the work (as expected of the team lead), covering all aspects: core programming, art, animations and VFX, balancing, even SFX. In retrospect, the whole thing was pretty ambitious, namely because of the sheer amount of content required to make an RPG as opposed to other genres. We managed to make a good number of assets to make the game respectable; let's just say the game is short and "meaningful", just how I like'em! ;) I also got the chance to receive valuable help from other experienced members, while my project also served as a sort of platform for new club members to practice on (one of them is now leading his own project as I write this). I had a blast all the way through, and while the game is definitely a beginner project, it's still something that I feel proud of and like to show others.
Sidenote: this game was made using Javascript/HTML5 on canvas. My next project will almost certainly be made with Unity2D or Unity 3D, depending on the idea I choose to stick with!!!

I might write a lengthier post-mortem on here at some point. For now, you can try out the game here! :D
