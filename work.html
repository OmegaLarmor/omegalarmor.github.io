I was given the chance to prove myself and landed a dream student job: working in an office setting

Professional growth at Great-West Life

For three summers, I worked at Great-West Life, a Winnipeg-based insurance company. Working in the Montreal office was a dream job for me: not only are the working conditions extremely advantageous when compared to "normal" student jobs, I also was given a good amount of responsability. My initial role was handling daily business such as mail and filing, and afterward my tasks were expanded to include data entry and printing request processing. Over those three summers, I had the chance to work with awesome people who believed in me and helped me adapt to working in a team environment. Everyone was very good to me, and I enjoyed my time there. There was only one thing they couldn't offer me, and that was incorporating science and technology in my work; for that I have had no choice but to look elsewhere, which is why I decided to move on and start my own journey as a programmer.
